# Spawn Protection

Spawn Protection creates a safe zone around your server's spawn point. Players cannot break blocks, place blocks, or cause damage in the protected area. This prevents griefing and keeps your spawn looking clean.

---

## Setting Up Spawn Protection

1. Open the **Admin Panel** (`!adminpanel`).
2. Go to **Spawn Protection**.
3. Configure the protection settings.
4. Save your settings.

The protection zone is centered around the world spawn point.

---

## Who Is Affected

- **Regular players** cannot build, break, or damage the spawn area.
- **Administrators** with bypass permissions can still interact freely with the spawn area.

---

## Configuration

From **Admin Panel > Spawn Protection**, you can configure the protection zone. The settings control the size of the protected area.

Adjust the radius to match the size of your spawn build. A larger spawn area requires a larger protection radius.

---

## Notes

- Spawn protection works independently of the Land Claims system.
- If you want to update your spawn point, use the standard Minecraft `/setworldspawn` command, then adjust the protection zone to match your new spawn location.
- Players who are stuck or need to leave spawn can always use `!rtp` to randomly teleport to a safe area outside spawn.
